# Code of Conduct

MindHandsHarness follows a simple contributor standard: be respectful, constructive, and specific.

## Expected Behavior

- Treat people with patience and good faith.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on evidence and trade-offs.
- Make room for contributors with different experience levels.
- Prefer clear, actionable feedback over vague criticism.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing discussions after maintainers ask to refocus.

## Reporting

If you need to report a conduct issue, open a private security advisory or contact the maintainers through the repository contact method once one is published.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when needed to keep the project healthy.
